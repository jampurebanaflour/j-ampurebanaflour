About Us

Introducing Our Banana Flour

J&AM PURE BANAFLOUR is a combination of the natural nutritional benefits of banana flour with the added goodness of moringa and kalabasa (squash). This unique blend offers a healthier, versatile alternative to traditional flours. It works wonderfully in a variety of recipes, from pancakes to bread and more, making it an excellent choice for anyone wanting to enhance their meals with added nutrition without compromising on taste or texture.

Vision

To be the leading producer of healthy, nutritious, and innovative banana-based flour products in the Bicol Region, known for our dedication to health, quality, and community growth.

Mission

To provide healthy banana flour products produced from locally grown unripe bananas, supplemented with natural, nutrient-rich ingredients like moringa and kalabasa.
